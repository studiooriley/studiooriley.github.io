Who am I?

If you’re here, you probably need a contractor to build you some outstanding digital content, and that’s exactly what I do.

My name is Liam O'Riley. I provide remote digital media services, from video editing and graphic design to illustration and writing. I hold a BA in Film/TV and an AS in Graphic Design from Walla Walla University.

What do I do?

After completing my degree, I began building my portfolio, which includes working on videos for organizations like Baker Boyer Bank, the Walla Walla Valley Chamber of Commerce, and two different running campaigns for the US House of Representatives.

In my work with organizations like Tangent Media Group, the Walla Walla University Church, the Blue Mountain Broadcasting Association, and the North American Division of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, I have performed nearly every role on the production team for both live and recorded projects, including:

On Set

  • Director
  • Cinematographer
  • Floor Manager
  • Grip
  • Actor

Production

  • Screenwriter
  • Consultant
  • Editor
  • Colorist
  • Motion Graphics Designer
  • Sound Editor

Off the production team, my work experience is all over the gamut. I’ve illustrated a children’s book for a small publisher, served as video consultant for the Northern New England Conference of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, and created a digital funeral service for a member of my local community. (In light of the COVID-19 pandemic, I have begun providing discount services for grieving families seeking memorial videos. If this is a service you would find valuable, please get in touch and we will discuss your needs, timeline, and budget.)

If you have a project or have questions about my services, visit my contact page and we’ll go from there. I look forward to hearing from you!
