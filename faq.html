What is Studio O’Riley’s response to the Coronavirus Pandemic?
The safety of our clients and ourselves is our foremost concern. Luckily, Studio O’Riley provides digital services, so most business is conducted remotely.

In the case of an in-person meeting, masks and social distancing measures are required. If sufficient COVID-19 precautions are not met by either party, the meeting will be rescheduled or canceled, at the discretion of one or both parties.

What film equipment does Studio O'Riley recommend for a budding filmmaker?
Camera

I love the Panasonic-Lumix GH4. While not as polished as the GH5, this camera delivers beautiful photos and videos at a reasonable price. It also records in 4K and sports a number of modern features like wifi, focus peaking, and multiple zebra settings. It’s only true handicap is its lack of an onboard image stabilizer.

Microphone

For Desks: The Blue Yeti Nano is my personal favorite microphone. It delivers crisp sound, both uni-directional and omni-directional recording, and can be bought for a little over $100.

For Sets: The Rode boom mic is a reliable, high-quality mic, used by many filmmakers in a wide variety of filming conditions.

Lights

Get yourself a color-adjustable light wand. These allow for the most flexibility for your money and a huge gamut of mood possibilities for your projects.
Is Studio O'Riley available for long-term projects?
Yes! We do this by offering project subscriptions. Each subscription is unique to your project’s needs. How does this work? We agree on a price.

  • We meet about your project and develop a weekly or monthly workload and methodology.

  • Each period, Studio O’Riley will do the work as agreed-upon and you pay your subscription.

  • The wheel turns on and on until the end of your project! (You can cancel any time.)
